## Retro Experiment
The experiment we decided on for this retro was for us to better organize our Kanban board by separating out big user stories and sub-issues into different columns and then have everybody assign themselves to an issue each week. This experiment would consist of labeling our user stories with the appropriate label and then putting them in the "User Stories" column. For sub-issues related to those user stories, we will put them into the "Ready" column where  everybody can assign themselves to an issue each week. We can then keep track of the issue on the Kanban board, making sure to move it to In Review and Done upon completion by the end of the week.

## Outcome
This experiment was largely a success. Our board and our issues was a lot more organized and everybody understood what issues were ready to be worked on. The team was able to keep each other updated on PRs, and we were able to more easily keep track of who had done what and what tasks we needed to work on ASAP.
