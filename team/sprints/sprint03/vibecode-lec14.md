## Our Vibe Coding Experiences
During our team meeting on vibe coding, we discussed our experiences using models and tools such as Gemini and Google Antigravity for the various coding tasks from HW03. These tools were most effective when we provided clear context, detailed step-by-step requirements, and images of what exactly we wanted from the UI. It was also helpful to describe to the AI tool what exactly each aspect or section the UI does, such as a scrollable list of furniture to place onto an apartment layout. Another thing that we found useful was asking the AI to first generate clarifying questions before proceeding with implementation. This really helped improve its results and got us a lot closer to what our exact requirements were.


## How We Will Incorporate It Into Project Development
Moving forward, we plan to incorporate vibe coding into our development workflow in a few different ways. We will use it to assist with repetitive or boilerplate tasks by applying the prompting techniques and models that proved effective. We also plan on having it help us with PR reviews. Specifically, we can use AI to double check pull requests for potential bugs, missed edge cases, or code simplification, serving as a second pass through the code. Additionally, we will use AI and vibecoding to help identify edge cases and find scenarios we may not have initially considered. Lastly, we will also incorporate vibe coding to help with documentation and summarizing all the features of our app, helping us maintain up-to-date information about out app and clearly communicate all aspects.
