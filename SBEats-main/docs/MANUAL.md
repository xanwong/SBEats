# SB Eats Manual

## Product Purpose
SBEats is a platform that makes discovering and sharing great food in the Santa Barbara area simple, engaging, and social. The app allows users to easily find nearby restaurants, view relevant details, and make informed choices based on ratings and community feedback. By allowing users to rank and rate restaurants, SBEats empowers them to capture their personal food experiences, while the ability to share rankings with friends adds a social layer that encourages recommendations, discussion, and discovery.



## Intended User Base
SBEats can be used by anyone in the Santa Barbara area, from longtime residents to weekend visitors. Thanks to UC Santa Barbara's presence in the area, SBEats also has a large number of features tailored to UCSB students.

## Usage Instructions

### Sign In / Sign Up
1. Open the SBEats app.
2. If you are a new user, tap on the "Sign Up" button and enter your required details.
3. If you have an existing account, enter your credentials and tap "Sign In".

### Discover Restaurants (Map)
1. The map view is accessible from the Home screen of the app.
2. Browse the map to see nearby restaurants represented by pins.
   - Users can also search for restaurants by name, cuisine, or keyword by using the search bar at the top of the Home screen.
3. Tap on a pin to view a quick summary of the restaurant.

### Search for Restaurants (List)
1. Go to the Search tab from the main navigation bar.
2. Type in the name, cuisine, or keyword related to the restaurant you are looking for.
3. Scroll through the filtered list of restaurants that match your search.

### Save Restaurants
1. Tap on a restaurant to open its detailed view.
2. Tap the "Save Spot" button to add the restaurant to your saved list.
   - Users can save restaurants by category.
3. You can view your saved restaurants anytime from your profile.

### Rate Restaurants
1. Select a restaurant you have visited to open its details.
2. Tap the "Rank Visit" button to open the rating section.
3. Select the rating you wish to give for each rating category.
   - Users can also note study spot perks the restaurant offers.

### Create a Post
1. Tap on the Posts tab from the main navigation menu.
2. Tap on the "Create" icon in the top right corner.
3. Add your caption and photos.
   - Users can optionally add hashtags and tag restaurants in their posts.
4. Tap "Post" to share with the community.

### Profile Features
1. Navigate to your Profile tab.
2. Here you can view your saved restaurants.
3. Tap on the Edit icon next to your username to update it.
4. See friend requests by clicking the "Friends" icon in the top-right corner.
