## HW04: Design

As part of the design effort, I plan to ensure the visual design throughout the app is consistent, also selecting a suitable main color as the brand identity for our app. I also aim to ensure the user experience is smooth and intuitive.

### Initial Starting Point
- Currently, our search page looks more like a simple list of restaurants rather than obvious clickable tags
- In the restaurant's page, the labels on the images are illegible. The information layout or hierarchy could also be improved.
- In dark mode, the current calendar page maintains its light mode color scheme, making it inconsistent.

### What HW04 Will Change
- In the search page, I plan to display each restaurants as tags rather than a list separated by lines
- In the restaurant page, I plan to either add a mask on top of the image for better contrast or categorise the image based on the labels. I may also restructure the page for better visual hierarchy
- In the calendar page, I plan to rework the UI so that it is still comfortably legible in a dark theme

### Updated: What HW04 Will Change
- In the restaurant page, I plan to restructure the information presented and improve the overall UI. A dark gradient will be added to the hero image to improve word legibility, and the words on the images below will be removed. The restaurant's general information will be grouped together, whereas user functions will be presented as buttons
- There will be a consistent branding implemented for the app, using a determined color palette, font, and spacing
- The number of pages on the navigation bar will be reduced so that it is less cluttered, and presents only pages with the highest expected traffic

As the app progressed, these changes were more neccessary than those not implemented as part of the intial plan.
