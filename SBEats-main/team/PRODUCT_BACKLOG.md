## User Story #1: Interactive Restaurant Map

As a user, I want to view restaurants on an interactive map and tap on a restaurant marker to open its profile page, so I can easily explore nearby options, understand where places are located, and quickly navigate to detailed restaurant information.

## User Story #2: Top Users Leaderboard

As a user, I want to see a leaderboard of the top users based on how many restaurants they have ranked and rated, so I can discover active contributors, feel motivated to engage more with the app, and build a sense of community and friendly competition within SBEats.

## User Story #3: Social features and group-based discovery

As a user, I want to follow friends, see a feed of their recent ratings and visits, so the app feels social and helps me discover spots through people I trust.

## User Story #4: Personalized discovery and recommendations

As a user, I want a personalized feed that recommends restaurants, dishes, or study spots based on my past ratings, saved places, and friends’ activity, so I can discover new places that match my preferences instead of seeing generic lists.
